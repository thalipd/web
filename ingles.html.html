WRITE 5 ADVICES FOR STUDENTS OF OUR SCHOOL ABOUT HOW THEY SHOULD TAKE CARE OF THEMSELVES

      1.Avoid being distracted by your cell phone, this can cause us to be robbed.
      2.Never talk to strangers or give information to these people as they can kidnap us.
      3.Take care of our belongings, keep them in a safe place.
      4.Always look around you if you are on foot, do not appear distracted.
      5.Inform your parents or family about the places you attend and where you plan to be so they can be aware of your safety.
